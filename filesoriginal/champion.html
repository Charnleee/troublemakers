THE MAN BEHIND THE GYM

FROM THE CAPE FLATS
TO AFRICA'S #1

Faeez "Troublemaker" Jacobs grew up in Mitchell's Plain on the Cape Flats — streets where the path most often led somewhere dark. He chose a different fight. Dubbed "Troublemaker" by a school principal for his rebellious spirit, he transformed the label into his identity and his armour.

"Troublemaker means to not conform, to be uniquely yourself and to choose to believe in yourself rather than shy away from things."

— FAEEZ JACOBS

He found MMA in grade 9 after spotting two kids wearing gloves around the corner from his home. Working as an Uber driver to fund his training, he turned professional with the EFC in 2017 and never looked back. He captured the EFC Bantamweight World Championship in 2019, ranked #1 in Africa, and has since defended and reclaimed the belt multiple times — including competing on an international main card at the Etihad Arena in Abu Dhabi.

In February 2026, Faeez headlined the sold-out EFC Knox Legacy Series at GrandWest Grand Arena in Cape Town — stopping Australia's Trent Girdham via Round 3 TKO to claim the belt for the third time. Every chapter of his career has been marked by resilience, hunger and an absolute refusal to stay down.

Beyond the cage, Faeez has built something equally remarkable — a coaching legacy. As founder and head coach of Troublemakers MMA & Fitness, he pours the same intensity and technical mastery that made him world champion directly into his athletes every single day.

HEAD COACH & MENTOR

BUILDING THE NEXT GENERATION

Faeez Jacobs doesn't just train fighters — he builds people. As the head coach and founder of Troublemakers MMA & Fitness, he brings the same discipline, technical mastery and mental toughness that took him to the top of African combat sports into every single session he runs.

His coaching spans the full spectrum. Children as young as five discovering coordination and confidence in the Kids BullyProof programme. Adult beginners stepping into a gym for the first time. Serious competitors preparing for amateur and professional bouts across EFC, Barebones, Rising Stars, WKS and beyond.

What makes him exceptional isn't just the world-champion knowledge he passes on — it's his ability to see potential before the athlete sees it themselves. He builds champions from the inside out. Champions are made here, not born somewhere else.
