WHO WE ARE

WELCOME TO TROUBLEMAKERS

Born in the heart of Palmstead, Cape Town, Troublemakers MMA & Fitness is more than a gym — it's a movement. We are a community of fighters, survivors, dreamers, and warriors who believe that the ring teaches you lessons life never could.

From complete beginners to seasoned competitors, we welcome every soul ready to transform. Our world-class coaches bring real-world combat experience to every class, ensuring you learn the right way — safe, effective, and powerful.
