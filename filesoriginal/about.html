OUR STORY

ABOUT TROUBLEMAKERS

WHO WE ARE

BORN TO MAKE TROUBLE

Troublemakers MMA & Fitness was built on one belief: that every single person has a fighter inside them — they just need the right environment to unleash it. Founded in Palmstead, Cape Town, our gym emerged from a passion for combat sports and a desire to give the community access to world-class training.

The name "Troublemakers" isn't about causing problems — it's about challenging limits. Making trouble for mediocrity. Causing chaos to excuses. Disrupting the idea that you can't. We're here to shake things up, and we want you to do the same.

Over the years, we've grown from a small, dedicated group of fighters into a full community gym serving hundreds of members — from children as young as 5 to adults across all walks of life. Our programmes span MMA, Boxing, Kickboxing, HIIT, Kids BullyProof, and our exclusive BoxFit for Ladies.

WHAT DRIVES US

OUR FOUNDATION

OUR MISSION

To provide the highest quality combat sports training in Cape Town — accessible to everyone, regardless of age, gender, or experience. We exist to transform lives through discipline, community, and the power of martial arts.

OUR VISION

To build Cape Town's most respected combat sports community — producing confident, disciplined, and capable individuals who carry the Troublemakers spirit everywhere they go.

OUR VALUES

Respect. Discipline. Perseverance. Community. Excellence. These five values guide everything we do, from how we train to how we treat each other. The gym is family — everyone belongs here.

WHAT WE STAND FOR

OUR VALUES

These aren't just words. They're the code we live by every single day — from the moment we step on the mat to the way we carry ourselves in the world.

JOIN THE FAMILY

RESPECT

Every person who walks through our doors is treated with dignity. We bow before we fight, and we support before we compete. Respect is the foundation of martial arts.

DISCIPLINE

The hardest fight is with yourself. We teach our members to show up, push through, and honour their commitments — on and off the mat.

COMMUNITY

We are more than a gym. We're a family. We celebrate wins together and support each other through losses. No one trains alone at Troublemakers.

PERSEVERANCE

Real growth happens when you want to quit and don't. We build fighters who refuse to stay down — in training and in life.

EXCELLENCE

We don't accept average. From coaching to conditioning, we pursue excellence in every detail — because you deserve nothing less.
