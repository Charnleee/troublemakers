BENEFITS FOR BODY & MIND

CARDIOVASCULAR HEALTH

Combat sports training is one of the most effective ways to improve heart health, endurance, and overall cardiovascular fitness.

MENTAL STRENGTH

Discipline, focus and resilience developed in the gym translate directly to success in every other area of your life.

STRENGTH & TONING

Full-body functional strength training that builds lean muscle, improves posture and makes you feel unstoppable.

SELF-DEFENCE

Real-world applicable skills that give you and your family the confidence and ability to protect yourselves in any situation.

WEIGHT MANAGEMENT

High-intensity combat training burns calories at an incredible rate. Achieve and maintain your ideal body composition.

COMMUNITY

Join a brotherhood and sisterhood of fighters who push each other, support each other, and grow together.

KIDS DEVELOPMENT

Discipline, respect, coordination and confidence for children aged 5-12 through our specialist BullyProof programme.

COMPETITION READY

For those wanting to compete, our coaches prepare fighters for amateur and professional bouts at the highest level.
